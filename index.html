Overview
Wingman is a trader training platform provided by Aperintel, offering simulated trading tools for educational purposes. This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal data and protect your privacy.
Data We Collect

Local Storage Only: All practice trade data, settings, and user preferences are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

API Keys and Credentials: Any API keys, authentication tokens, or credentials you provide are stored only in your browser's local storage. These are never sent to Aperintel servers. They are sent directly and exclusively to the respective third-party API provider as needed.

Analytics: We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics to understand platform usage patterns and improve features. No personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or analysed. Individual user behaviour is not tracked.

Third Party Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your local data remains entirely under your control.

Your Rights and Data Control

Under GDPR and similar data protection regulations, you have the following rights:

Right to Access: You can view all your data stored locally at any time through your browser's developer tools or by exporting it.

Right to Delete: You can permanently delete all your personal data by clearing your browser local storage or using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.

Right to Correct: You can edit or update any personal information (such as trader name) at any time in Settings.

Right to Withdraw: You can stop using the platform at any time and your local data will remain on your device until you choose to delete it.

Cookies and Local Storage
We use essential browser local storage and cookies for app functionality only, including session management and user preferences. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third party analytics cookies. No profiling or behavioural tracking is performed.
Security
Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system updated, using a strong device password, and clearing local storage when using shared devices.
Contact and Complaints
If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact Aperintel. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.
This policy applies to Wingman provided by Aperintel. The policy will be updated as the platform develops. Continued use of Wingman constitutes acceptance of this privacy policy.
