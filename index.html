COT: Institutional Positioning
SOURCE: CFTC · WEEKLY (TUE DATA)
Commitment of Traders report shows how Commercial hedgers (usually right at extremes), Large speculators (trend followers, follow when extreme), and Small speculators (retail, contrarian) are positioned. Data is delayed 3 days, published every Friday for the prior Tuesday.

Options Flow
SOURCE: CBOE · 15MIN DELAYED
Put/Call ratio below 0.7 = bullish (more calls being bought = market expects up move). Above 1.0 = bearish. Above 1.3 = extreme fear, often a contrarian buy signal. Unusual options volume flags where smart money is positioning.
